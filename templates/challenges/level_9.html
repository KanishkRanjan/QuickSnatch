Scheduled Tasks Investigation

Your mission is to investigate scheduled tasks and cron jobs to find a hidden flag. You'll need to understand how cron works and where to find relevant information.

Your Tasks:
  • Examine crontab entries
  • Check system logs for cron job execution
  • Investigate scheduled task outputs
  • Monitor temporary files created by cron jobs

Hint: Cron jobs often write their output to log files or temporary locations. Check both the cron configuration and its execution traces.
